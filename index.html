Travel Info

🇦🇺 from australia

PASSPORT:
PLEASE ENSURE YOU HAVE A PASSPORT WITH AT LEAST 6 MONTHS VALIDITY LEFT ON IT FOR INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL.

ESTA:
THIS IS NECESSARY FOR FOLKS ON AN AUSTRALIAN PASSPORT

IT IS A SIMPLE, ONLINE APPLICATION WHICH YOU CAN COMPLETE AND COSTS ABOUT $14. EACH PERSON MUST HAVE AN APPROVED ESTA PRIOR TO TRAVELLING TO THE USA. AT TIMES THE ESTA MAY TAKE UP TO 72 HOURS TO APPROVE. THE VALIDITY OF THE ESTA IS 2 YEARS FROM APPROVAL DATE

IF YOU HAVE AN EXISTING ESTA, PLEASE CHECK THIS IS STILL VALID AND WILL BE VALID FOR WHEN YOU TRAVEL CLOSER TO SEPTEMBER 2019.

READ ABOUT AND COMPLETE YOUR ESTA HERE
https://esta.cbp.dhs.gov/esta/
